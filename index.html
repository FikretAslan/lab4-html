Pandas: Why do people love them?

It is never that easy to spot a panda. The factor that this animal is super rare makes it very popular. The popularity comes by since everyone gets amazed after setting eyes on it. The amusement of seeing a giant panda comes to another extent, whether its the 1st time you are seeing it or not. In the wild, there are only 1600 giant pandas. A good number of giant pandas are in captivity, mostly in zoos. The total number of giant pandas in captivity ranges above 300 in China.
